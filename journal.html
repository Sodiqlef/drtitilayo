Open Journal

Welcome to my "open journal" where I will share tips and inspirations for "wholesome" living.

Life is an exciting journey where we constantly make discoveries and learn.

In the clinic where I work as a pediatrician, I realize that parents often need help knowing what to do for their kids and sometimes are misguided by the plethora of health information available online and other informal sources. When I identify these health-knowledge gaps, I convert them into valuable tips and guides for mothers and caregivers so that we can have less childhood morbidity and mortality attributable to ignorance.

As a mother-in-waiting, I have acquired wisdom for the "lonely" journey. I learn daily from casual conversations, books, teachings, and the Holy Spirit. If you are a mother-in-waiting, this is a good place for you. I will share my thoughts, discoveries, and experiences with you whenever I learn something new, and we can connect and support each other.
