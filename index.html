About Me

I am a pediatrician, storyteller, and happy wife.
When I am not practicing medicine, I love to read, write, and sing.

My Mandate

To raise an army of “wholesome” children.
“I am a deliverer out of Zion!” Obadiah 1:21

God has given me the mandate to raise an army of "wholesome" children. I fulfill this through my different expressions. As a pediatrician in Abuja, Nigeria, I actively evaluate, diagnose, and manage various childhood illnesses.

I engage in health education and advocacy programs that create awareness and provide parents and caregivers with health information, helping them make informed decisions about their children's health.

I teach children between the ages of one and fourteen years in my local church assembly and have authored two books.
